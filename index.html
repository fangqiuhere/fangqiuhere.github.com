VeCaN

Lab of Vehicle Control and Networking, short for VeCaN, focuses on the national long term science development planning. VeCaN is dedicated to vehicle dynamics, the transport network and the control and decision-making problems in the field of new energy.

Tongji

Tongji University (simplified Chinese: 同济大学; traditional Chinese: 同濟大學; pinyin: Tóngjì Dàxué), colloquially known as Tongji (同济, Tóngjì), located in Shanghai. Established in 1907 by the German government together with German physicians in Shanghai, Tongji is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in higher education of China.
